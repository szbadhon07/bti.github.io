About

We started our Company in 2009 from this time. Badhon Trade International is a famous and popular name in the Handicrafts sector. The peoples of Bangladesh know BTI for its authentic and beautiful handmade textiles. We sell our products not only in Bangladesh but also in abroad. As Badhon Trade International. First, we attend the International trade fair 2004. Through the trade fair, We gain more popularity of Bangladeshi people abroad. We received a presentation National Association small and cottage Industries of Bangladesh fair 2007 from NASCIB.

Our mission is to curate beautifully handcrafted products from across the globe. We preserve the traditional art of Bangladesh handed down to us by our ancestors, helping our talented artisans gain global recognition through the products that can be used daily in the urban market.

Our vision is to be an International Brand synonym to craft. We are well on our way with a solemn goal to provide economic upliftment of the rural artisans involved in preserving the heritage of the handicrafts industry in Bangladesh.
